Experimental SAXS data for twelve IDPs: the N-terminal region of pertactin (PNt) (Riback et al., 2017), the NR interaction domain of N-CoR (CoRNID) (Cordeiro et al., 2019), two deletion mutants of Tau (K19 and K25) (Mylonas et al., 2008), the "plug" domain from a TonB-dependent receptor (FhuA) (Riback et al., 2017), Î±-synuclein (aSyn) (Ahmed et al., 2021), the low-complexity domain of hnRNPA1 (A1) (Martin et al., 2020), the T-domain of colicin N (ColNT) (Johnson et al., 2017), Sic1 (Gomes et al., 2020), the activation domain of ACTR (ACTR) (Kjaergaard et al., 2010), Histatin-5 (Hst5) (Jephthah et al., 2019) and a tandem repeat of Histatin-5 (Hst52) (Fagerberg et al., 2020).
